Family Guy
Family Guy is one of the best comedies of all-time. It's about the Griffin family (Peter, Lois, Stewey, Meg & Chris) and their talking dog Brian. I don't even know how to explain how much I absolutely love this show! It has been a laugh out loud funny show for almost 20 years now!
About
The Griffins is an American animated sitcom created by Seth MacFarlane for FOX in 1999. To date, the series is one of the few television series to have been stopped and brought back due to popular demand, and it is also one of the few series to have been rebooted production by the same company that stopped it.
